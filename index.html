HungerStation Fraud Detection

Download the policy installer for your browser and run it to auto-install and enable the extension.

Important: You must fully restart your browser after running the policy helper. Close every browser window, end any remaining processes in Task Manager (e.g., chrome.exe, msedge.exe, brave.exe), then re-open the browser. Otherwise the extension will be automatically disabled.
  1. Choose the policy installer for your browser (Chrome, Edge, or Brave).
  2. Download and run the .bat file as Administrator (right-click → Run as administrator). This tells your browser to allow and auto-enable the extension.
  3. Fully restart your browser. The extension will auto-install and enable automatically after restart.
